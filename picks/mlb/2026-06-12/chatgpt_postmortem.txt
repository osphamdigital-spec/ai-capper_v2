## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

PROPOSED CHANGE:  
Reduce stake size by one tier when lineups are unconfirmed **and** the edge is driven primarily by aggregate lineup advantage or bullpen superiority rather than a clean starting-pitcher edge.

WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER:  
I was willing to press ATL to 3 units and LAD to 1 unit even though both writeups depended heavily on offense/bullpen edges while lineup cards were still unconfirmed. When the starting-pitcher case is not dominant, lineup uncertainty should matter more.

EVIDENCE FROM TODAY (cite pre-game data, not the outcome):  
- ATL was the best-bet mostly on “much stronger offense vs RHP” and “best bullpen in this slate,” while lineups were unconfirmed.  
- LAD was supported by “major platoon edge” and “weak opposing bullpen,” but my own writeup admitted the Dodgers starter was not a pristine profile.  
- SEA, by contrast, was kept to 1 unit because I explicitly respected the small-sample pitching uncertainty. That restraint was methodologically cleaner.
