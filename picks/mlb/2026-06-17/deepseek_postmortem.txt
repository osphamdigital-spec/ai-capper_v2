### S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL (to my own method document)

**PROPOSED CHANGE:**  
*Do not place any bet whose edge is heavily dependent on a platoon split unless the exact batting order and individual platoon wRC+ for each hitter are confirmed. If lineups are unconfirmed, either cap the bet at 1 unit or treat the team’s split as league‑average until the order is known.*

**WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER:**  
Platoon‑split edge bets must be grounded in the specific hitters that will actually face the opposing starter, not a roster‑wide average. The CHW bet failed because the method used an aggregate team number (128 wRC+) that was wholly unrepresentative of the nine men who played.
