## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL
**PROPOSED CHANGE:** Introduce a “lineup certainty” discount when team‑level platoon wRC+ is the primary edge driver and the actual lineup is not yet confirmed. When the edge exceeds 15 points, reduce the units deployed by one (e.g., 3u → 2u) unless the probable lineup can be verified from pre‑game reports. Additionally, for any bet with a wRC+ gulf >20 points on one side, cross‑check whether that team has recently rested or lost key hitters – if so, double‑check that the aggregate stat still reflects the expected nine.  
**WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER:** The method correctly identifies value but implicitly assumes the season‑long platoon number holds for that day’s nine hitters. A simple lineup‑aware adjustment would protect against bets like MIA today, where the actual lineup undercut the edge dramatically.  
**EVIDENCE FROM TODAY:** MIA’s team wRC+ vs LHP (126) was over 20 points higher than the lineup that actually took the field (106). That gap was invisible to the pre‑game process that reported “no data gap.” A modest stake reduction would have limited the damage.  
**CONFIDENCE:** MEDIUM — one day is not enough to set a firm rule, but the pattern is clear enough to justify testing a formal adjustment.
