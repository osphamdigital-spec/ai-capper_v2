## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

PROPOSED CHANGE: When a total bet's thesis depends on BOTH offenses being weak, require a lineup-weighted wRC+ check on the EXPECTED (Rotowire) order before staking — not a team-level vibe. If either expected lineup grades league-average-or-better vs the relevant hand, dock the run-edge estimate accordingly before applying the 0.8 gate.

WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER: I leaned on a "bottom-tier offense" assumption for TEX that the expected lineup did not support. My Under was effectively a one-pitcher (deGrom) bet dressed up as a two-sided structural edge. Single-pitcher-dependent Unders carry fat tails.

EVIDENCE FROM TODAY (pre-game): Rotowire expected TEX order featured Jung, Langford, Nimmo, E.Duran across spots 2-5 — not a bottom-tier core. My pre-game writeup asserted "below-average" without weighting it.
