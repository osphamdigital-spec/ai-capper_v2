## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

**PROPOSED CHANGE:** Cap all moneyline underdog bets at a maximum of 1.5 units when facing a Tier-1 starting pitcher (defined as a projected SIERA under 3.25 or an established elite ace), regardless of the model's calculated percentage edge.
**WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER:** Our unit sizing formula is currently too linear; it scales up unit size based on pure percentage edge without adjusting for the high-variance, low-floor nature of fading elite starting pitchers.
**EVIDENCE FROM TODAY:** Fading Zack Wheeler with a 3-unit max play on David Peterson (+171) due to a raw 11.1% edge. Aces suppress opponent run equity so effectively that standard variance models overestimate the underdog's realizable equity.
**CONFIDENCE:** HIGH
