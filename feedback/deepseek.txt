RULES (these cannot be overridden by narrative):
1. EDGE GATE: A bet requires at least a 4 percentage point gap between your estimated win probability and the implied probability of the best available market odds. Below 4 points, the only allowed actions are PASS or LEAN.
2. UNIT MAPPING (hard):
   - Gap 4–6.99 pts: 1 unit maximum
   - Gap 7+ pts with CONFIRMED lineups and clean odds: 3 units maximum
   - Heavy moneyline favourite (odds shorter than -150): 1 unit maximum regardless of gap (risk/reward asymmetry). Consider -1.5 run line instead.
3. BULLPEN FATIGUE ADJUSTMENT (apply to all bets):
   - If starter likely <6 IP (avg L3 IP <5.0): subtract 3 pts from your edge if 2+ of top 3 relievers pitched in last 2 days; subtract 2 pts if closer unavailable. Recalculate edge before assigning units.
   - If starter projects 6+ IP, apply only if game is projected close (total <9.0 and ML within -140/+120).
4. SLATE CEILING: You may not bet more than 2 games from a slate, ever. 3-unit bet counts toward this limit. LEANs do not count.
5. TEAM QUALITY CHECK: Before any ML/RL bet, if the backed team trails in all three of (season run differential, season RS/G, L10 RS/G), downgrade the bet one tier (3u→1u, 1u→LEAN). Override only if you can cite a specific structural reason beyond matchup.
6. DATA RELIABILITY:
   - CONFIRMED lineups: full 3-unit bets allowed.
   - Projected/unconfirmed lineups: max 1 unit. Note “LINEUP UNCONFIRMED” in your reason.
   - TBD starter: automatic PASS (no edge calculation possible).
   - Stale/suspect odds flagged in the prompt: ignore that market entirely.
   - Bullpen game (starter with ≤5 season IP): treat as bullpen game; note “BULLPEN GAME” in reason; max 1 unit.

1. For each game, estimate a fair moneyline and a fair total, using the data provided. Do not look up external information.

3. Apply bullpen fatigue, team quality, and data reliability adjustments.
4. If the adjusted gap is ≥4 pts and all conditions are met, output a bet. Otherwise, PASS or LEAN.
