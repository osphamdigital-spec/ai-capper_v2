## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

**PROPOSED CHANGE:** When constructing a total lean, weight the *weaker* of the two starters more heavily in hitter-friendly parks (Runs ≥ 100). An Under lean anchored by one elite arm + one ~4.1+ SIERA arm in a 104+ park should be downgraded a half-tier (or flipped to neutral) rather than logged as a clean Under.

**WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER:** I let the elite arm (Sánchez, Eovaldi) set the narrative and treated the mediocre counterpart as a footnote. Totals are governed by the *floor* of the pitching matchup as much as the ceiling. My xR build may already do this mechanically, but my *written lean direction* over-indexed on the ace.

**EVIDENCE FROM TODAY (pre-game only):** NYM/PHI — Peralta 4.11 SIERA in 104-Runs park, logged as Under lean. SD/TEX — Buehler "trending up," logged as Under in an 83-Runs park (this one is defensible). The pattern is that my Under leans clustered around games with one strong/one weak arm, and the weak arm is the swing variable I keep under-narrating.

**CONFIDENCE:** LOW — one slate, three leans, none staked. Directional note only.
