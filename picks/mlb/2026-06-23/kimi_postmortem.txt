## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

PROPOSED CHANGE: Regress individual hitter platoon wRC+ toward league average (100) when the sample is <30 PA in that split, then re-aggregate the team offensive projection before feeding it into run totals.

WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER: My current team platoon wRC+ aggregates treat a 124 wRC+ built on 1, 24, and 52 PA the same as a 124 built on 200-PA samples. That can inflate offensive projections when a lineup is carrying multiple tiny-sample outliers.

EVIDENCE FROM TODAY (cite pre-game data, not the outcome): 
- BOS @ COL: The pre-game snapshot showed BOS vs-LHP wRC+ of 124, but it was constructed partly on Eaton (1 PA), Wong (24 PA), and Monasterio (52 PA). 
- CLE @ CHW: The CHW vs-LHP wRC+ of 121 included Perez (6 PA), Montgomery (12 PA), and Romo (20 PA). 
Using these aggregates raw overstates the hitting talent level without penalizing hitter-side uncertainty symmetrically to how [sm] flags penalize pitcher uncertainty.
