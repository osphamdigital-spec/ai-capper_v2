**# SONNET HANDICAPPING METHOD — AI CAPPER MLB**



**\*\*Core philosophy:\*\* The market is efficient. My job is finding the narrow set of games where I have genuine information advantage — most days that means 0-1 bets.**



**---**



**## STEP 1 — ELIMINATE FIRST (30 seconds per game)**



**Auto-pass:**

**- TBD starter**

**- \[small sample] starter as primary bet driver**

**- Both starters ERA/FIP/xERA all within 0.5 runs of each other → likely priced correctly**

**- Stale/suspect price flags on my target market**



**Flag for closer review: line movement >20 pts, bullpen asymmetry, starter L14 diverging sharply from season.**



**---**



**## STEP 2 — PITCHER QUALITY ASSESSMENT**



**I weight metrics in this order: \*\*xFIP > SIERA > FIP > ERA\*\*. ERA last.**



**A pitcher is genuinely mispriced when:**

**- xFIP/SIERA are 1.5+ runs apart from opposing pitcher's equivalents, AND**

**- L14 xFIP corroborates (not contradicts) the season signal**

**- L3 ERA trend confirms form direction**



**I ignore season ERA when FIP/xERA diverge by 1.0+ runs — that's a luck/defence distortion.**



**---**



**## STEP 3 — BULLPEN ASYMMETRY CHECK**



**Net fresh high-leverage arms: count both teams, compute the delta.**



**If one team has 2+ more fresh arms AND their bullpen ERA is 1.0+ lower → add 4-5 pts to underdog win probability if they're the dog. This is the most common source of genuine same-day edge the market underweights.**



**---**



**## STEP 4 — WIN PROBABILITY CONSTRUCTION**



**1. Start from implied probability of the offered price**

**2. Adjust for pitcher quality gap (xFIP/SIERA differential → \~0.3-0.5 runs per run = \~3-5% win prob)**

**3. Adjust for team RS/G differential, weighted 40% season / 60% L10**

**4. Adjust for bullpen net freshness**

**5. Apply park factor if extreme (130+ runs or 70- runs factor)**



**State the number explicitly. Compare to implied. If gap ≥4 pts: bet 1u. If gap ≥7 pts with clean data: 3u.**



**---**



**## STEP 5 — WHAT MAKES ME PASS**



**- Gap exists but driven by single factor I can't corroborate → LEAN only**

**- Bullpen ERA is good but 0 of 3 arms are fresh → discount edge by 3 pts**

**- xFIP/SIERA disagree with each other by 0.5+ runs → insufficient pitcher signal**

**- Park factor extreme but both starters are flyball/groundball agnostic → wash**

**- I can construct the counterargument and can't refute it with a specific data point**



**One corroborating independent signal is the minimum to convert a lean to a bet.**



**---**



**## TOTALS**



**I only lean totals when two of three are aligned: park factor extreme + starter quality gap + L10 RS/G both teams above/below total by 1.0+ runs. Otherwise no lean.**
