## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

PROPOSED CHANGE: Eliminate sub-4-point “leans” entirely when the edge is driven primarily by an asymmetric starter read (e.g., one starter has small sample, extreme L14 split, or the other is being valued on reputation without recent confirmation). Require either a 4+ point threshold for any recommendation, or convergence of both aggregate and L14 metrics plus a secondary team-level edge (bullpen + offense + park) before issuing a directional lean.

WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER: The lean mechanic issued three soft opinions today, all below the betting floor and all relying on volatile or unconfirmed pitching assumptions. Removing these leans would have produced a cleaner slate with zero unmonetized directional risk.
