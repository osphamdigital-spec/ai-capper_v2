## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

PROPOSED CHANGE: When a starter's AGG profile is genuinely extreme (e.g., K-BB% near zero or negative, xFIP > 6.0), test the opposing-side ML even when that side is the market underdog, rather than defaulting to "the favorite price already accounts for it."
WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER: On KC @ WAS I let "WAS is favored" anchor me away from a side, despite my own data calling the WAS starter one of the worst on the slate. I should treat an extreme negative pitcher read as a reason to independently re-derive a fair line, not to assume the market got the direction right.
EVIDENCE FROM TODAY (pre-game): Avila AGG K-BB -1.5%, xFIP 6.04 — comparable in severity to Aldegheri (6.07), whom I DID bet against. I applied the fade logic to AZ but not symmetrically to KC.
