## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL

PROPOSED CHANGE: When choosing between ML and run line (-1.5), require a higher coverage threshold before taking the RL at plus-money — the RL adds correlated variance on top of an already-thin edge.
WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER: On COL @ CHC I had only ~5.5 pts of edge and converted that into a -1.5 RL needing ~55% coverage. A starter-quality edge often produces a win without a 2-run margin; taking ML (-191 implied) vs RL (+100) trades coverage for price. I leaned to price without fully accounting that the RL doubles down on the same variance I was already thin on.
EVIDENCE FROM TODAY (pre-game): My own note priced RL-cover at ~55% vs +100 — a 5% margin, the thinnest of my four bets, yet structurally the highest-variance bet form. The edge math didn't justify the variance multiplier.
