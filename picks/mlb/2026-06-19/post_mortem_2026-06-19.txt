For most teams, this is appropriate — a 108 wRC+ lineup is roughly consistent regardless of which specific players are present on a given day. When that player rests or is injured, the team's actual offensive projection for that specific game can be 15-25% lower than the season aggregate suggests. My method does not currently have a systematic trigger to re-evaluate when a named key player is absent.
