## S6: MODEL-SPECIFIC REASONING INSTRUCTION

MODEL: Kimi (kimi-k2.6)  
REASONING WEAKNESS IDENTIFIED: Over-reliance on team-level platoon splits (wRC+ vs LHP/RHP) without sample-size validation, and allowing a single noisy split to drive the majority of the required 4-point edge.  
PROPOSED PROMPT INSTRUCTION:  
*"Before using any team wRC+ split (vs LHP or vs RHP) as a primary betting input, confirm the split is based on at least 250 team PA in the current season. If the sample is below 250 PA, regress the split 50% toward the team's overall wRC+. Do not allow a platoon gap alone to constitute more than one-third of the required 4-point edge gate; the remaining edge must come from starting pitching (SIERA/K-BB%), bullpen quality, or defensive metrics."*  
WHY THIS ADDRESSES THE WEAKNESS: It prevents the model from anchoring to volatile team splits (e.g., TEX 140 wRC+ vs LHP, SD 81 wRC+ vs RHP) and forces the edge to rest on more stable, pitcher-driven inputs. This directly stops the false-edge construction that produced the losing TEX recommendation.  
SOURCE OF THIS INSIGHT: Post-mortem of 2026-06-13 slate where a 1-unit play lost due to an unregressed platoon split and resulting inflated probability gap.  
EXPECTED IMPACT: HIGH
