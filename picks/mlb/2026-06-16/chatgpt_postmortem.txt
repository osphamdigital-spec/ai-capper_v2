## S4: METHOD CHANGE PROPOSAL
PROPOSED CHANGE: Reduce the weight of team-level platoon splits when lineups are unconfirmed, and cap stake size unless projected/confirmed lineup-level split strength agrees with the team number.

WHAT IN MY METHOD COULD BE SHARPER: I let season team split data carry too much of the offensive case in a few games where batting-order quality was the actual swing factor. If the edge depends on “Team X crushes LHP” or “Team Y is weak vs RHP,” I should demand lineup-level confirmation before turning that into a real-money position or a strong lean.
