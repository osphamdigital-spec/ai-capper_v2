**METHOD — OPUS MLB v1**



**CORE STANCE**

**The 9-book median is my prior and I treat it as sharp. I do not "rate teams" — I hunt for the specific spot where a stable, identifiable signal is not yet in that price. Absent such a signal, I pass. My deviation from implied probability is proportional to how acute and recent my edge is: season-level reads the market has also seen move me 1–3 points at most; acute recent or structural signals can move me further.**



**PITCHING (largest input)**

**I ignore single-season ERA as a true-talent input and distrust season xERA/FIP under \~40 IP. My starter baseline is the larger-sample AGG xFIP and SIERA, anchored by Stuff+ (>105 = real whiff stuff, <92 = contact-dependent). I then adjust for form using L14/L3 — but only when L14 IP is meaningful (≥9 IP); an elite or ugly L14 built on 3 innings is noise I discard. A bet appears when ERA-anchored market pricing diverges from a stable AGG read in the direction L14/L3 confirms.**



**BULLPEN**

**"High-leverage arms fresh: X of Y" plus taxed flags is my second lever. A 0-of-N fresh pen behind a starter unlikely to finish 6 is a real, frequently underpriced fade — strongest on totals (Over) and on live dogs in close projected games. I treat implausible bullpen ERAs (e.g. 22+) as data artifacts and ignore them.**



**OFFENSE / PARK**

**Platoon team-aggregate wRC+ vs the listed hand frames lineup quality but is supporting context only — never primary justification, since lineups are unconfirmed at build time. Park and wind drive totals: Coors and HR-inflated parks (Camden HR 115) push Over; 15+ mph wind matters by direction.**



**WIN PROBABILITY**

**Blend projected starter runs-allowed (from AGG estimators) + bullpen quality/fatigue + offense-vs-hand + park into an expected run margin; compress to win prob (≈12–15 pts per projected run of margin around 50%, capped near .380/.620); compare to implied. Bet only on a ≥4-pt gap.**



**RUN LINE**

**Only with an independent P(win by 2+) estimate that exceeds the RL implied probability — never justified by the moneyline.**



**PASS WHEN**

**TBD starter; edge rests only on season data the market has seen; estimators agree with ERA and the price is fair; heavy chalk with a fair derivative; Coors sides (high variance — I lean totals there, not ML).**
